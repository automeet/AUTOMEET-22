ABOUT US

AUTOMEET'22

AEA is one of the student body association of the Department of Automobile Engineering responsible for conducting the National level technical symposium AUTOMEET.

This symposium is aimed at boosting the technical knowledge of students by having workshops, technical events of various technologies in the Automotive Industry. AEA in association with RAA (Royalz Alumni Association) help students grow in their career with the vast and strong alumni who are at leading positions at various locations across the globe.
